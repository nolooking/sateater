Introducing nolooking


Members:


nolooking for easy lightning onboarding

If you follow the usual setup process for a new lightning node, you must first fund your new node using an on-chain transaction. After first depositing to your node you must wait for several ~10-minute confirmations, and then you can open channels one at a time.

Opening each channel costs on-chain mining fees, and makes you wait for confirmations between successive channel opens. Plus it is difficult to know when you have sufficient connections.

Making Lightning Channels Easier and More Private
